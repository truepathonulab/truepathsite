About our project

Every of us spend about 72 hours per year in traffic jams. It's terrible. In the 21st century people can't affort such "luxury". Our social project's goal is to build automated rational distribution of traffic flows.

How do we achieve this

Collect and analyze data from CCTV

Includes building of typical routes depending on weekday, part of the day, season, etc.

Build rational routes

Based on information about routes typicality, road congestion and surface condition the rational for the majority of drivers route is built.

Distribute flows

With traffic lights and other regulatory signs with special software our system strongly recommends drivers to move along the built route.

What in result?

We will help all drivers to reduce the time spent on the road

the amount of emissions into the atmosphere will decrease and the ecological condition will improve

Drivers’ overall mental state will be improved and stress levels will be reduced
